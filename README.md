FocusLens AI is a privacy-first, gamified Chrome Extension designed to help you regain control of your time and stay focused on your goals.

## ğŸš€ Key Features

- **Privacy-First**: All your data stays locally in your browser. No servers, no tracking.
- **Goal-Based Tracking**: Select your current goal (Study, Coding, Fitness, etc.) and let the AI categorize your browse behavior.
- **Credit Economy**: A unique gamified system where you earn credits for productive work and spend them on distractions.
- **Interactive Interventions**:
  - **Soft Lock**: A blurred overlay with a countdown timer when visiting distracting sites.
  - **Hard Lock (Bankrupt)**: Blocks distracting sites entirely when you run out of credits.
- **AI Insights**: Real-time analysis of your focus patterns and multitasking habits.

## ğŸ® How to Use

1. **Set a Goal**: Click the extension icon and choose your current focus (e.g., *Coding*).
2. **Earn Credits**: Spend time on productive sites (GitHub, StackOverflow, etc.) to earn **1 credit every 5 minutes**.
3. **Spend Credits**: Distracting sites (Netflix, Instagram, etc.) cost **1 credit per minute**.
